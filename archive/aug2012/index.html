campusvine

Campusvine is a labor of love. At Heuretix, we have spent the past few months intensely working on Campusvine. So what is Campusvine? If you are a college student, it's nearly impossible to find out what is going on around campus. Right now the options suck, its either you go to the library and look at this mess and try to filter the signal from noise or you actually browse through the spam on Facebook.

It is easier to understand what Campusvine is by understanding what it is not. Campusvine is not an uninspired attempt at digitizing the mess. Campusvine is not an idiotic attempt to compete with Facebook. Most attempts are merely attempting to solve the problem by ironically recreating it. Campusvine will be represent a paradigm shift in the discovery of events on a college campus.
