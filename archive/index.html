Campusvine

A collection of apps for higher education.

Background

I spent a lot of time in college doing stuff in the student government. That's where I met Alex. First, we worked on Allocate. Allocate was a Rails app for student clubs to send spending requests to the student government. The first attempt was a flop, but we learned a lot. The second attempt was good, and the student government used it for a couple years.

As we were leaving college, we co-founded Heuretix to continue building web apps for higher ed. In the Summer of 2012, we rewrote Allocate from scratch. We are currently working on a couple projects. One is super-secret, the other, an events website, you can check out here.

Nooklyn

The best way to find apartments in Brooklyn.

Background

When we were looking for an office for Heuretix in Brooklyn, we asked Nooklyn to help us out. They found us a great loft right off of the Jefferson Street L stop. In late 2012, they were looking for someone to work on their website. I thought it would be neat side project and conscripted David to work on it with me. We hated their old website, so we rewrote it in Rails and launched it within a month. We work on it from time to time, adding features and functionality as they come up.
